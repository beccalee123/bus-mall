How to Vote

We're excited to announce BusMall, the digital, ground transportation version of everyone's of everyone's favorite catalog, SkyMall! To get ready for our launch, we need your help choosing the best products to feature in our inaugural issue. As a thank you for your participation, we'll be offering you 50% off of your first BusMall purchase.

To vote, please click your favorite product in each group of three shown to you. You'll be given 25 votes, and at the end of your voting session, you'll be able to check out the data for your favorite products. Don't worry if you see the same products pop up throughout the survery - we've designed it that way so we can see how each product stack up against its competition.

Thanks for helping us bring the best quality products to BusMall!
